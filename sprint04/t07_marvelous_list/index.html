Pirates of the Caribbean 1

August 21, 2003

Johnny Depp

Geoffrey Rush

Orlando Bloom

Keira Knightley

The film tells the story of Captain Jack Sparrow
who rescues Elizabeth Swann, daughter of Governor Swann,
from the crew of the Black Pearl, captained by Hector Barbossa.
Elizabeth is seeking a pirate to return her to the mysterious
Isla de Muerta, and Jack wants revenge on Barbossa,
who mutinied against him. The crew of the Black Pearl
attack Port Royal and kidnap Elizabeth. Will Turner,
a blacksmith and friend of Elizabeth, teams up with Jack to save her.

Pirates of the Caribbean 2

July 7, 2006

Johnny Depp

Orlando Bloom

Keira Knightley

Jack Davenport

The film tells the story of Captain Jack Sparrow
who is being pursued by an undead sea captain,
Davy Jones, who is seeking to end his life.
Jack is joined by Will Turner and Elizabeth Swann
who are looking for Jack's compass, which will lead them
to a key that will unlock a chest containing a valuable
heart that Davy Jones needs to end his curse.

Johnny Depp

Orlando Bloom

Keira Knightley

Geoffrey Rush

The film tells the story of Captain Jack Sparrow
who is being pursued by the East India Trading Company
for his role in the escape of the notorious pirate
Captain Hector Barbossa. Jack is joined by Will Turner
and Elizabeth Swann who are looking for Jack's compass,
which will lead them to a key that will unlock a chest
containing a valuable heart that Davy Jones needs to end his curse.
